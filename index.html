Become a Music Master

Music Master is a powerful audio tool that allows you to take music into your own hands and unleash your creativity.

It's built for Game Masters, allowing to craft dynamic, event-driven soundscapes that react to your story. Layer tracks, automate fades, and trigger cinematic moments with a click.

Music Master

Other apps give you canned soundboards.
Music Master gives you full creative control.

• Import your own MP3/WAV/OGG files.
• Layer tracks and crossfade seamlessly.
• Automate pitch, tempo, and volume changes.
• Build custom triggers with the visual node editor.
• Edit on desktop, play on tablet.
• Works fully offline.
• No subscriptions.

Features

🎶

Multi-Track Mixing

Play ambience, music, and SFX all at once to create rich, layered soundscapes that bring your world to life.

✂

Track Cutting

Cue precise moments in your tracks effortlessly and jump to exactly the right mood at the perfect time.

⚡

Event-Based Control

Create your own custom events and trigger them with a single click when the story demands it.

🎛

Automation

Fade, crossfade, and change pitch or speed over time to create dynamic, evolving soundscapes.

📱

Cross-Platform Ready

Seamlessly transfer projects between desktop and tablet so you can edit anywhere and play everywhere.

🌐

Offline Friendly

Works anywhere, even without the Internet, so your game night is never interrupted by connectivity issues.

👑

One to Rule Them All

Single license works on multiple devices, or share with friends to create collaborative audio experiences.
